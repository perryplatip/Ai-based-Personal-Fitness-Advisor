Jumping Jacks

Start with your feet together and arms at your sides, then jump to a position with legs spread and arms overhead. Return to starting position and repeat.

Benefits & Instructions expand_more

Benefits: Improves cardiovascular fitness, works entire body, increases coordination.

  1. Stand with feet together and arms at sides
  2. Jump while spreading legs and bringing arms overhead
  3. Return to starting position in one fluid motion
  4. Repeat at a moderate to fast pace

Tip: Keep a slight bend in your knees to reduce joint impact.

5 minutes

High Knees

Run in place, lifting your knees toward your chest as high as possible while pumping your arms. Focus on speed and height.

Benefits & Instructions expand_more

Benefits: Strengthens hip flexors and core, improves coordination, increases heart rate.

  1. Stand with feet hip-width apart
  2. Run in place, bringing knees up to waist level
  3. Pump arms in opposition to legs
  4. Maintain an upright posture with core engaged

Tip: Aim for height rather than speed at first, then increase tempo as you build stamina.
